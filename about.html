Welcome to Icel Steps Learning

Our History

Icel Steps Learning was founded in 2019 by a dedicated teacher who made the difficult decision to leave the classroom—not out of choice, but out of necessity. When her son was diagnosed with dyslexia, she realized that even the best schools around lacked the resources and expertise to support children with learning disabilities. Determined to provide her son with the education he deserved, she embarked on a journey of homeschooling and self-learning, delving deep into strategies that cater to unique learning needs.

What began as a personal mission soon evolved into a larger vision—to create a safe, inclusive, and empowering learning space where children with learning difficulties are not left behind. This passion led to the birth of Icel Steps Learning, a platform designed to bridge the gap in education by offering specialized support for children with dyslexia and other learning challenges .

Today, Icel Steps Learning is more than just an institution; it is a beacon of hope for learners who often go unnoticed in traditional education systems. With tailored programs, a nurturing approach, and a commitment to innovation, the platform ensures that every child, regardless of their learning abilities, can unlock their full potential.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To be a leading center of excellence in inclusive education, where every child, regardless of their learning challenges, receives the support they need to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally in a nurturing environment.

Our Mission: To provide accessible, personalized, and inclusive education that empowers children with autism, dyslexia, and other learning differences to reach their full potential. Through specialized teaching methods, compassionate support, and a commitment to every learner’s success, we strive to bridge educational gaps and create lifelong opportunities
